F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need create an account to use Smart Life?

You don't need to create an account to use Smart Life but you may miss out on a lot of useful features. Having a Smart Life in Fife account gives you complete access to all of our assessments, health and living advice, product recommendations and more. Creating an account is also a great way to keep an eye on your progress. All of your past assessments can be viewed through the account section and you can also mark items for future use.

How do I create an account?

To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" link in the top menu. Using the "Create a New Account" form, enter your username and password. After reading the terms and conditions, press the "Sign up" button (provided that you agree).

What sort of personal information do you keep & what happens to any information I submit?

We only store information that you send us, such as your name, email address and your ADL Platform results. We do not share your details with third parties except where relevant and you will be notified if and when this occurs. For more information please see our privacy policy.
